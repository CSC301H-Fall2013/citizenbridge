    • Vision

    CitizenBridge would like to create a relationship between citizens and representatives. The current government web page is complicated to use and documents are hidden away. CitizenBridge seeks to create a system that would simplify access to documents and allow citizens to interact with their representatives.

    Team Charter

    Purpose

    We, the members of Team Athabasca will work together on improving the CitizenBridge website by providing a more accessible, impactful and personalized user experience for constituents of Canada.

    Members

    Values

    We, the members of Team Athabasca promise to uphold the following values as respect to all team members and the project, CitizenBridge.

    1. Teamwork
    2. Friendship
    3. Honesty
    4. Integrity

    Commitments

    We, the members of Team Athabasca promise to uphold the following commitments as respect to all team members and the project, CitizenBridge.

    1. We will meet a minimum of two times every week.

      1. a. Tuesdays after CSC301 lecture
      2. b. Thursdays during CSC301 tutorial
      3. c. Emergency meetings on Saturdays

    2. We will commit to replying to any e-mail within 24 hours.

    3. We will work to the best of our abilities in any task assigned to or volunteered for.

    4. We will notify other members of any emergencies that will affect our ability to complete assigned tasks as soon as possible.

    5. We will assist each other through any problems that we face in the course of this project.

    6. We will commit to a friendly and positive working environment for all members.

    7. We will commit to the keeping the reporting plan up-to-date and well organized.

    Reporting Plan

    In order to communicate progress, Team Athabasca will use Trello software. All tasks will be placed, updated and archived appropriately. TODO and DOING sections will contain appropriate items. A separate list will be used for each day's DONE tasks.

    Supporting Resources

    In the event of difficulty, our Tutorial Assistant and Professor will provide assistance.

    Scope of the Project

    The scope of the project is the addition and improvement of features for the Constituent accounts. Representative account features are not included in the scope of this project.

    Technology

    Php, Codeignitor, W.E.T (Web Experience Tool kit), JavaScript
